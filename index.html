Marketing is the process of moving all products and services from the concept phase to the consumers. Many think it is about providing value to the customers. It might seem easy to outsiders, but marketing is an important and complicated process. Usually, marketing entails working with the 4 P’s. The 4 P’s of marketing are product, price, promotion and place. But, there is much more to marketing than these.
